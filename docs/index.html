How it works

1

One phone hosts

Pick a game on any phone with Ubapp. It puts up a QR code and a short app code.

2

Everyone joins

Friends scan the QR in their browser, or open Ubapp and type the code. No app needed for browser guests.

3

Play in the room

Everything runs over the local network on the host phone. No servers, no accounts, no data leaves the room.
